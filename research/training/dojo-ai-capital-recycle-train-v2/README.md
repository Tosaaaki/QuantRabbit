# DOJO AI capital recycle TRAIN v2

Six 2026-H1 cases were preregistered before model calls. Each judgment ran in
one isolated context with no tools, and all six raw first attempts were sealed
before the answer key was generated. The model could only keep the existing
position, cut it, and accept or reject one new directional candidate. A fixed
hierarchical allocator then applied `KEEP = full HOLD` and
`FLAT + valid candidate = full rotate`; the model did not choose size.

The policy is rejected. It produced -27.4 capacity-pips, 6.5 pips better than
always holding (-33.9) and 19.8 pips better than always rotating (-47.2), but
0.6 pips worse than cutting to reserve (-26.8). Existing-direction accuracy was
2/6, new-candidate direction accuracy was 3/6, and joint accuracy was 1/6.

This is a small, self-attested, worn-history TRAIN diagnostic. It is not a
global untouched holdout, proof, promotion evidence, live authority, or a
month-level profit result. Its cross-pair raw pips are a normalized direction-
policy score, not JPY P/L: pair-specific pip value, margin, financing, and
portfolio correlation are not modeled. The exact evidence and content-
addressed bindings are in `evidence.json`; the full immutable trajectory
remains in the external DOJO archive `codex-ai-capital-recycle-train-v2`.
